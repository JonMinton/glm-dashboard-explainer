Different Kind of Outcome

Unlike Tutorial 1 where we predicted a continuous variable (heart rate in bpm), here we're predicting a binary outcome: does the patient have heart disease? (yes/no). This requires a different type of GLM.

Step 1: Select the Response Variable

Drag Heart Disease to the Response zone on the right.

The response variable (y) is what we're trying to predict or classify.

Why this variable? We want to classify whether patients have heart disease based on their diagnostic measurements.
